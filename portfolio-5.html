Titans Paying It Forward

Fundraising Landing Page

Description

  • Titans Paying It Forward

  • www.TitansPayingItForward.org

The official fundraising landing page of the non-profit; The Albert Einstein High School Athletic Hall of Fame, who provides scholarship opportunities to deserving student athletes.

The client wanted a simple design that told a story, and had a quick call to action. This App was designed for use on all mobile platforms.
